RoboTax

Fill in the fields below to calculate the required yearly tax on an automated system in order to ensure that the government income tax does not drop when a company changes to an automated system.

1. Employees to be Replaced

  • # of Employees
  • Average Tax ?Tax cost per employee per year.
  • Salary ?The amount the employee makes annually.
  • Productivity ?Units produced per day.

2. New Hires

  • # of Employees
  • Average Tax ?Tax cost per employee per year.
  • Salary ?The amount the employee makes annually.

3. Automations

  • # of Automations
  • Cost ?Initial cost of the machine.
  • Productivity ?Units produced per day.
  • Life Span ?Life Expectancy of Automation.
